Welcome to the webpage for Harry Jain's senior thesis in CS and Math!

This website presents a mixture of research and analysis from my thesis in an interactive format. Take a look around to get an idea of some of the most interesting takeaways, simulations, and more. The pages are organized as follows:

  • Home: This page, which gives a brief overview of the project and descriptions of the other pages
  • About: A section of documentation regarding the project, its goals, and some key takeaways
    • Project Proposal: The original proposal for the project, as submitted in Fall 2022
    • Midterm Presentation: A presentation given in Winter 2022, which gives an overview of the project and some preliminary results
    • Final Math Presentation: The final presentation for the Math department in Spring 2023, which gives a more in-depth look at the math behind the project
    • GitHub Repository: The GitHub repository containing all the analysis code utilized for this project and this website
  • Background: A section addressing previous research on measuring streaks in sports, with a rebuttal of some misconceptions and an application of related research to NBA games
    • The Hot Hand: A mathematical rebuttal of the supposed "Hot Hand Fallacy" using the research of J.B. Miller and A. Sanjurjo
    • Streak Measures: An overview of some potential mathematical measures of streaks and desirable properties outlined by Zhang, Bradlow, and Small
  • Simulations: A section including several simulations for various statistics and results regarding NBA streaks
    • Measure Simulations: Statistical significance tests for a variety of streak measures described in the Background section
    • Streak Simulations: A comparison of streak length proportions under the assumption of constant Elo ratings with the actual streak lengths of teams
  • Predictions: A listing of today's games with win probabilities according to the current (gap-adjusted) Elo rating
  • Conclusions: The most important takeaways from the project, along with references

As with all great teams, this project is under constant construction, so bookmark this page to follow along with progress and learn all about streaks in the NBA...
